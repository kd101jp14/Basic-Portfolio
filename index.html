About Me

Hello there! I am Kyra. I am a Vanderbilt Full Stack Boot Camp student. I have only just started the course, and I have already learned so much. I enjoy learning about web applications and finding issues in code.

Before joining this program, I graduated from Vanderbilt University with a degree in Psychology (Research Concentration). After graduating, I took an opportunity to teach English in Japan. Since My time in Japan, I have had the opportunity to work for a startup tech company. There, I worked very closely with developers. I found their job very interesting. However, I was later laid off from working at that startup company when they changed their company structure.

Due to these recent experiences, I have taken the opportunity to make a career change and become a developer! I am very excited to learn web development and coding at this point in my life. Now, I must balance coding with my other interests, such as cooking, spending time with family, and taking care of my pet turtle!
