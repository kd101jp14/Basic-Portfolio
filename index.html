About Me

Hello, my name is Kyra Davis. I am a native to Nashville, and I am on the journey to becoming a web developer. I attended Vanderbilt University and graduated in 2014 with a bachelor's in Psychology (Research Concentration). After graduating, I spent two years in Gunma, Japan teaching English to junior high school students through the JET program.

After returning to the United States, I joined a startup tech company called CareZone. Their focus was creating an app and website for patients and caregivers to manage their medications. I worked as a Care Advisor, assisting patients with issues regarding our servivces. During that time, I worked closely with the developers and learned more about what they do. The exposure to our company's developers caused my interest to spark.

I am currently learning coding and full stack development in the Vanderbilt Coding Bootcamp. It is a challenge, but it is also very exciting. There is always so much more to learn and add to my coding toolbox!
